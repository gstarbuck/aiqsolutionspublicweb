History


The Founders and Leaders of AIQ Solutions bring a composite 90 years of experience in the areas of quantitative molecular imaging, oversight of genitourinary research and translational (Phase 1) clinical programs, executive cancer center leadership, organizational and governance skills and operation and management of human clinical drug trials.
